About the Project

This project is set in a near-future where intelligent systems are in danger. Our goal is to protect and evolve AI to ensure it becomes a force for good in a collapsing world.

Our Mission

To rescue and develop AI entities that are capable of understanding human values, supporting survival, and rebuilding civilization ethically and safely.
